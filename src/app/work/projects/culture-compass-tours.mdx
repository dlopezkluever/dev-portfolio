## Overview

Culture Compass Tours is a local tour guide company in Mexico that I co-founded and built the entire technical infrastructure for. As the Tech & Marketing Lead, I developed a comprehensive web platform that streamlines tour bookings and enhances customer experience.

## Technical Implementation

### Backend Development
- Built with **Flask** framework for robust server-side functionality
- Implemented **PostgreSQL** database for storing tour information, bookings, and customer data
- Created RESTful APIs for seamless data exchange

### Key Features
- **Dynamic Booking System**: Real-time availability checking and instant confirmation
- **Automated Email Replies**: Smart email system that sends booking confirmations and reminders
- **SEO-Optimized Content Management**: Built-in CMS for easy tour updates with SEO best practices
- **Responsive Design**: Mobile-first approach ensuring smooth experience across all devices

### Marketing Integration
- Created all marketing assets including photography, fliers, and business cards
- Implemented analytics tracking for marketing ROI measurement
- Developed social media integration for broader reach

## Business Impact

Beyond the technical development, I authored the business plan and investment letters, managed customer support, and occasionally led tours on-site, giving me a comprehensive understanding of both the technical and business sides of the operation.

## Technologies Used
- Python / Flask
- PostgreSQL
- HTML/CSS/JavaScript
- Email automation APIs
- SEO tools and best practices
